I am an R&D Intern in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, working with Prof. Koustav Rudra. My current work builds a question-answering and multiple-choice dataset from cryptocurrency YouTube video transcripts, and I coordinate the cryptocurrency information retrieval track that IIT Kharagpur is organising at the Forum for Information Retrieval Evaluation (FIRE 2026).

I am largely self-taught. I started programming on a smartphone at 13, building chatbots for companies while in school, shipped a full-stack application from my phone at 17, and independently published my first research paper at 20, now cited across IEEE, IOP Science, and NCIT venues.

I am a third-year B.Tech (Computer Science) student, open to research opportunities.
