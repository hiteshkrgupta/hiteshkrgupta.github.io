I am currently an R&D Intern in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, under Prof. Koustav Rudra. My current work builds the QA and multiple-choice dataset from cryptocurrency YouTube video transcripts, and I coordinate the FIRE 2026 shared task on cryptocurrency information retrieval (IIT Kharagpur).

I am largely self-taught. I started programming on a smartphone at 13 — building chatbots for companies while in school — shipped a full-stack application from my phone at 17, and independently published my first research paper at 20 (now cited across IEEE, IOP Science, and NCIT venues). An engineer moving into full-time research, I am drawn to hard problems in AI diagnostics, distributed systems, and software that holds up in production. I am a third-year B.Tech (Computer Science) student, open to research opportunities.
